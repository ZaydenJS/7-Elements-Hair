Our Sustainable Practices

Every aspect of our salon is designed with environmental responsibility in mind

Waste Recycling

Through our partnership with Sustainable Salons, we recycle hair clippings for oil spill cleanup, foils for manufacturing, and chemicals for safe disposal.

  • Hair clippings recycled
  • Foils repurposed
  • Chemical waste safely processed
  • Packaging materials recycled

Water Conservation

We implement water-saving techniques and use eco-friendly products that require less water for rinsing and processing.

  • Low-flow fixtures
  • Efficient washing techniques
  • Water-conscious products
  • Mindful usage practices

Eco-Friendly Products

Our exclusive use of DeLorenzo products ensures all treatments are vegan, cruelty-free, and made with sustainable ingredients.

  • 100% vegan formulations
  • Cruelty-free testing
  • Sustainable packaging
  • Australian made

Ponytail Donation Program

We're proud to facilitate ponytail donations that help create wigs for people experiencing hair loss due to medical conditions. Your hair can make a real difference in someone's life.

Donation Requirements:

  • Minimum 14 inches (35cm) in length
  • Hair must be clean and dry
  • No chemical damage or excessive processing
  • All hair colors and textures accepted

Sustainable Salons Partnership

We're proud members of Sustainable Salons, Australia's leading salon sustainability program. This partnership allows us to divert 95% of our salon waste from landfills.

What Gets Recycled:

  • Hair clippings → Oil spill cleanup
  • Foils → Manufacturing materials
  • Chemicals → Safe disposal
  • Packaging → Recycling programs
