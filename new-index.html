Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for color services and weekend appointments. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings.

What should I expect at my first visit?

Your first appointment will include a comprehensive consultation where we discuss your hair goals, assess your hair condition, and create a customized treatment plan.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we accept various payment methods including Afterpay for services over $100. Please ask about our payment options when booking.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand life happens! Please give us at least 24 hours notice by calling or texting to avoid any fees.
